    Awards

    Best paper

    Awarded to "A MIREX Meta-analysis of Hubness in Audio Music Similarity" by Arthur Flexer, Dominik Schnitzer, and Jan Schlueter, from the Austrian Research Institute for Artificial Intelligence (OFAI)

    Award: 1 iPad

    Best student paper (Google sponsored)

    Awarded to "Score Analyzer: Automatically Determining Scores Difficulty Level for Instrumental e-Learning" by Véronique Sébastien, Henri Ralambondrainy, Olivier Sébastien, and Noël Conruyt from the University of Reunion Island

    Award: 500$

    Best oral presentation (Cycling'74 sponsored)

    Awarded to Jean-Julien Aucouturier (IRCAM and LEAD Lab, University of Burgundy, Dijon) and Emmanuel Bigand (LEAD Lab, University of Burgundy, Dijon) for "Mel Cepstrum & Ann Ova: The Difficult Dialog Between MIR and Music Cognition"

    Award: 1 Max6 licence

    Best poster presentation (Cycling'74 sponsored)

    Awarded to Pablo Sprechmann (University of Minnesota), Alex Bronstein (Tel Aviv University), and Guillermo Sapiro (University of Minnesota) for "Real-time Online Singing Voice Separation from Monaural Recordings Using Robust Low-rank Modeling"

    Award: 1 Max6 licence

    Best tapper to Western music (Ableton sponsored)

    Awarded to Erdem Unal

    Award: 1 Max4Live licence

    Best tapper to Turkish music

    Awarded to Olmo Cornelis

    Award: Turkish delight...
