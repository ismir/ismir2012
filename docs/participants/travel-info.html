    Travel info

    Airport

    Porto is served by Francisco Sá Carneiro Airport (OPO) - [Link] [Google Maps Location]

    Francisco Sá Carneiro Airport is a modern infrastructure, recently renewed, located just 15 min. away (by car) from the city center and the conference venue.

    Several companies fly to Porto, including low cost, include direct destinations to e.g. Newark, Montreal, São Paulo, Frankfurt, Paris, London, Amsterdam, Madrid, etc.:

    • Aigle Azur 
    • Air Berlin 
    • Air Transat
    • Brussels Airlines
    • Easyjet
    • Iberia
    • Lufthansa
    • Luxair
    • Ryanair
    • Sata International
    • TAP Portugal
    • TAAG
    • Transavia
    • Royal Air Maroc 

    Train station (traveling from e.g. Lisbon, Madrid, etc.)

    Estação de Campanhã (Porto) - [Link] [Google Maps Location]

     


    View ISMIR 2012 Venue and Walking Distance Hotels in a larger map

    From the airport to the conference venue

    The best way to get from the airport to the conference site is using the Metro (follow the ). Average waiting time at the airport is 15 minutes. Ticket type is a Z3, costing 1.20 €. The trip takes about 20 minutes. You travel on the E line (violet), in the direction of Estadio do Dragão, and leaving at station Trindade, at a 5-minutes walk from the conference venue. (An optional 2 stations travel on the D line, until São Bento station will get you even a bit closer.)

    To go to your hotel, you can check the Metro and bus networks to find the best route. Note that a number of recommended hotels are at walking distance to the conference site (check the map). 

    Moving around

    Porto has a Metro () and a network of buses (). Trains (CP) from the two main train stations (Campanhã or São Bento) will help you to plan any trip to the outskirts.
